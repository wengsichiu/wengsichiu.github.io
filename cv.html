Projects

  • Green Eats | Oct 2014-present
    Initiated a campaign to reduce food waste in local restaurants by raising awareness and encouraging conscious consumer behaviours, such as by taking away leftovers and bringing reusable containers. This campaign started in Montreal and operates through social media and partnerships with restaurants.
  • RePack | Oct 2012-May 2014
    Initiated and organized a project with students and McGill residence supervisors to collect unwanted frosh (student orientation) backpacks from students to send to a local school in rural Thailand. The aim of this project was to prevent students from throwing away the backpacks and to put them to better use. The backpacks arrived at the school in May 2014.
