Graphic Designing

Illustrations. Logos. Infographics. Campaigns.

Designs are something that we come across and are affected by everyday. They have the power to influence our decisions including how we consume products and ideas and which companies to work with. They are also an important factor in raising awareness and bringing attention to certain topics - from healthy eating to food waste to global conflicts. I hope to create a positive change in the world through creative graphic designs. Check out some of my work.

Free Web Design

Designing sites for local projects and start-up organizations

A website can impact an image of a company, organization, or a brand. For a while I have been creating websites with Weebly and HTML using templates. I've recently created websites for local projects in Guatemala, an organization based in India, and initiatives here at home in Toronto. Now I'm developing my HTML skills for the more real stuff.

This is something that I really enjoy doing and would like to gain more experience in. I would be happy to create free sites for socially-minded projects and businesses. Check out some of the ones I've made in the past. If you want to learn more, get in touch!
