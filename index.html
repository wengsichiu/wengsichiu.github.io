Graphic Designing

Illustrations. Logos. Infographics. Campaigns.

We come across and are affected by designs everyday. They have the power to influence our decisions including how we consume products and ideas, and which companies we work with. They are also an important factor in raising awareness and bringing attention to important topics - from healthy eating to food waste to global conflicts. I hope to create a positive change in the world through creative designs. Check out some of my work!

Free Web Designing

Designing sites to promote local projects and start-up organizations

A website can have a big impact on the image of a company, organization, or a brand. For a while I have been creating websites with Weebly and some HTML to help promote projects and organizations. I've recently created websites for local projects in Guatemala, an organization based in India, and initiatives here at home in Toronto. Now I'm developing my HTML skills for the cool stuff.

This is something that I really enjoy doing and would like to gain more experience in. I would be happy to create free sites for socially-minded projects and businesses. Check out some of the ones I've made in the past. If you want to learn more, get in touch.
