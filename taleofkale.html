The Tale of Kale

Children's Book Illustrator


Illustrating a children's book about a boy who grows to love kale is one of the coolest experiences I've had in design. I was helping out with the Borden Team at Borden Communications and Design Inc. in Toronto and this happened to be a project they were working on. Of course I said I would give it a shot and after the course a few weeks, we came up with something that we were both happy with.

All these designs started off as hand-drawn sketches. Using Adobe/illustration software and a bit of creativity, these pictures below are some of the outcome. I'm hoping they help inspire kids to eat healthy and to try different foods like kale over and over again, because it might just become their favourite!

Check out The Tale of Kale website for more info
