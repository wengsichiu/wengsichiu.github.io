A bit about me

Homemade Graphic Designer. Web Designer. Digital Nomad.

I'm Wengsi, a recent grad working on the go and finding new opportunities along the way. I love to travel and fall in love with new places. If you find me at home in Toronto, I'm spending quality time with family and friends while planning my next adventure and building up new skills. Graphic and web designing are some of my hobbies/ side hustles.

Skills I'm currently working on include graphic designing, HTML, and Spanish. I recently returned home from Mexico City and that's where I hope to end up finding opportunities with these skills. Meanwhile, I'm working and volunteering from Toronto with a few organizations around the world that are trying to make it a better place. I hope to contribute to that through my creativity and designs.
