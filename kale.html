"Tale of Kale" illustrations

The Tale of Kale, written by Lisa Borden, is a children's picture book based on a real story and meant to be a colourful and happy way to encourage good eating habits.
I illustrated this book using hand-drawn sketches, Inkscape, and Adobe Shape softwares. You can find this book on Amazon by clicking here.
